Services

Total Solution Logistic Service

V.J.B. Logistics possesses total fleet of delivery-vehicles with variety types (4, 6, 10 and 18 wheel) and specifications (temperature control and non-temperature control) with safety lock. All V.J.B.’s vehicles are plugged with GPS satellite to checking goods status and report back to company. In addition, all vehicles are carefully maintenance and daily check-up for cleanliness and safety of both workers and customer goods. Our customers can ensure that they will receive satisfaction with our service since we provide:

  • New and safe vehicles with maintenance team support
  • Carrying weight of goods under Thai’s regulation
  • Goods Insurance*conditions apply
  • Responsible for the late service or emergency
  • 24 hour service, 7 days a week
  • The containers are all temperature controlled and can be monitor via our GPS system.
