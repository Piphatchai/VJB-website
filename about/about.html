About Us

VJB SERVICE CARRENT

V.J.B. Logistics Co., Ltd. is a fully integrated logistics service provider in Thailand, offering our partner highly flexible and reliable logistics service. Established in 2006, the company possesses strong regional network, reliable transmit time, modern logistics system and database, with highly skilled logistic workforce.


Our services include total solution logistic service, warehousing and distribution service, labeling and repacking service. We are specializing in transportation of high-value products and temperature sensitive goods including healthcare and medical products, consumer goods, electronics and computers, and so forth.

VISION V.J.B.

Logistics Company Limited operates under its visions to become leader in the logistics industry providing our customer with greater flexibility and reliability. Empower by our commitment towards service quality and cost effective solutions adapted to meet the changing needs of our customers.
